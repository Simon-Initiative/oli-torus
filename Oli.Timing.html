    run(func)

    View Source

    Runs a function and times its execution. Returns a tuple of the form {time, result} where time is the time in microseconds and result is the return result of the function.

    Examples 

    iex> run(fn -> "3" end)
{2, "3"}

iex> run(fn a, b-> a <> b end, ["Bob", " Ross"])
{3, "Bob Ross"}
